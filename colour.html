Colour

Issue

A strong colour contrast between text and background is always recommended. However, different users respond differently to different colours.

Solution

Allow users to choose a colour scheme that suits them.

Exercise

Use the toggle button in the top right corner to choose between a “Light Mode” and a “Dark Mode.”

Press "n" to go back or "m" to continue.
