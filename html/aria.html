ARIA

When semantic HTML is not descriptive enough, use ARIA labels and roles.

Consider the “hamburger menu” in the top left corner of the screen. For icons with no accompanying text, add an “aria-label” to the HTML element, describing what functionality the icon stands for.

Exercise

Write an aria-label describing the functionality of the hamburger menu.

Your answer:

Sample answer: aria-label="Navigation menu"
